Event Management

At Armin's Catering, we don't just provide mouth-watering food - we provide a full event management service so that you don't have to worry about a thing on your special day. We work closely with you in the run-up to your event so we know exactly how you want your celebration to go and at every event we have a dedicated floor manager at all times to ensure all goes well from start to finish. This allows you to relax and enjoy yourself, secure in the knowledge that everything is in hand for a fantastic experience for you and your guests.

We start by inviting all clients for a food tasting where you get to taste a wide variety of our delicious food plus any special dishes you have requested. 95% of our customers who come for a food tasting place an order with us based on the high standard of our food. We ensure that we work to a budget that suits each individual client and we are always happy to help clients to select a suitable menu to ensure they get maximum value for money.

Once a booking is made, we visit the venue and survey the place to ensure we bring the right equipment on the day. We provide a full banqueting service which includes:

  • linen
  • crockery
  • cutlery
  • glasses
  • chair covers and coloured sash of your choice
  • table decorations.

If you require further assistance for your event, we can also recommend providers for:

  • flowers
  • disco
  • the bar

If you would like to ask us any questions or to book an event please get in touch with us. We look forward to hearing from you.
